    • PLAYDATE TERMS AND CONDITION:

    1. All children must be accompanied by a FULLY Vaccinated guardian at least 18 years of age.

    Please read the following terms and conditions carefully. By entering the playdate, you and all members of your group agree to abide by these terms and conditions:

    1.Playtime Duration: The allotted time for the playdate is 2 hours and 30 minutes. You may still extend playtime for an additional fee, but not in the party room.

Please respect other guests and maintain a pleasant environment within the party room. Please be reminded that only food from Doña Milas will be allowed for this package.

    7. Amendments: We reserve the right to make amendments to these terms and conditions as necessary. Any changes will be communicated to the participants in advance.
